## UI Gaps

### Gap: AC11 — Transfer dialog has no fee-component selection to prevent incorrect totals

**Layer:** Frontend
**AC:** AC11 — Prevents incorrect total amounts; only valid services are reallocated
**What is missing:** The Transfer modal moves the full event record and does not support selecting individual fee components (Waste Fee, Transport Fee, Eco Fee) to exclude from the transfer within the transfer flow itself. Fee component inclusion is managed separately in the Component Billing section of the event detail page (toggling individual fee checkboxes), but these two actions are disconnected. A user who transfers an event and forgets to adjust the Component Billing section afterward may inadvertently invoice incorrect fee totals at the new customer. The two-step process (transfer first, then adjust fee components separately) is not guided or enforced by the UI.
**UI surface needed:** The Transfer modal (TransferEventModal.jsx and BulkTransferModal.jsx) should include an optional "Fee Components to Transfer" step that shows the Waste Fee, Transport Fee, and Eco Fee checkboxes from the Component Billing section, allowing the user to deselect any fee that should not follow the transfer. Alternatively, a post-transfer prompt should remind the user to review Component Billing settings on the transferred event.
