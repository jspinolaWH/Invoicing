## Acceptance Criteria Coverage

| # | Criterion | Status | Notes |
|---|-----------|--------|-------|
| 1 | The system supports reverse charge VAT invoicing (VAT liability transferred to buyer) | IMPLEMENTED | Product.reverseChargeVat flag, VatCalculationService, Invoice.reverseChargeVat field all present and wired |
| 2 | Automatic application when a product/service marked as subject to reverse VAT is added | IMPLEMENTED | VatCalculationService.isReverseChargeApplicable() checks product flag & customer businessId; InvoiceGenerationService applies 0% rate and sets invoice flag |
| 3 | Single invoice can include both standard VAT and reverse charge VAT (mixed) | IMPLEMENTED | FinvoiceBuilderService.groupLinesByVatRate() + resolveEffectiveVatRate() applies 0% only to RC-product lines; VatGroup entity handles mixed grouping |
| 4 | Invoice includes legal references, buyer's VAT number, and reverse charge indication | IMPLEMENTED | Legal notice added as InvoiceFreeText; BuyerPartyIdentifier VATNumber in FINVOICE; "Reverse Charge" badge in UI; VatCode "RC" on rows |
| 5 | Reverse charge VAT transactions included in VAT reports | IMPLEMENTED | VatReportService aggregates standard VAT and reverse charge base totals; VatReportPage displays both with date-range filtering |
| 6 | Reverse charge VAT information exported in FINVOICE format | IMPLEMENTED | FinvoiceBuilderService exports buyer VAT number, legal notice (InvoiceFreeText), and RC vat code per row |
| 7 | Auditability and traceability — system records all invoices with reverse charge VAT | IMPLEMENTED | Invoice.reverseChargeVat persisted in DB; VatReportService counts RC invoices; BillingEventDetailPage shows per-event reverse charge status |
