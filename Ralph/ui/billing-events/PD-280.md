## Where to Find This Feature

This feature lives under the **Customers & Config** tab. Property Groups are configured in the **Property Groups** sidebar item, where shared service arrangements are created and participant percentage shares are defined. The resulting invoice lines (one per subscriber) are visible in the **Invoice List** under the **Invoices** tab.

## User Flows by Acceptance Criterion

---

### AC1 — Individual invoice lines per subscriber; 0–100% shares; total must equal 100%; warning if not; option to exclude 0% participants

1. Click **Customers & Config** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Property Groups**.
3. You will see a table listing all property groups. Each row shows the group name, number of participants, the current total share percentage (coloured green if valid, red if not), and a validity indicator.
4. Click the **Manage** button on any property group row. A detail panel appears on the right side of the screen.
5. In the detail panel you will see a coloured banner at the top showing the current total share percentage and whether it is valid (green "Total: X.XX% ✓ Valid") or invalid (red "Total: X.XX% ✗ Must equal 100%"). This is the live warning when the total is not exactly 100%.
6. The participant table shows each subscriber row with fields: Customer Number, Share % (editable number field, accepts 0–100), Valid From, Valid To, and an **Include if 0%** checkbox that controls whether a participant with a zero share appears on the invoice.
7. If you change shares so the total is not 100%, the banner immediately turns red and the **Save Participants** button is disabled.
8. When the total equals exactly 100%, the banner turns green and the **Save Participants** button becomes active.

**Confirms requirement:** The UI enforces the 0–100% per-participant constraint and shows a real-time error banner when the total deviates from 100%, and each row has an "Include if 0%" toggle.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC2 — Default equal split; user-defined agreements; exported invoice entries include shared service, % share, total cost

1. Click **Customers & Config** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Property Groups**.
3. Click **Manage** on any property group to open the detail panel.
4. To apply the default equal split: click the **Equalise** button. The system automatically recalculates each participant's share so they are equal (rounding the last participant to make the total exactly 100%).
5. To set user-defined shares: manually type different percentages into the **Share %** field for each participant row. The banner updates in real time.
6. Click **Save Participants** to store the custom distribution.
7. To verify the invoice output: click **Invoices** in the top navigation bar, then click **Invoice List** in the sidebar. Click on any invoice that involves a shared service property group. In the invoice detail, the line items table shows each participant's line. The description field contains the participant's percentage share (e.g. "25% share"), and a small "Service total" sub-line displays the total net cost of the shared service.

**Confirms requirement:** The Equalise button provides the default equal-split mechanism; manual editing allows user-defined agreements; the invoice detail shows shared service identity, percentage share, and total service cost.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

### AC3 — Shared service implemented as property groups

1. Click **Customers & Config** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Property Groups**.
3. You will see the Property Groups page — this is the UI surface for defining shared services in the system.
4. Click **+ New Group** in the top-right corner. A modal appears with fields for **Name** and **Description**.
5. Enter a name (e.g. "Building A Shared Waste Service") and click **Create**.
6. The new group appears in the table. Click **Manage** to open its detail panel and add properties/participants.

**Confirms requirement:** Shared services are configured as named property groups through this dedicated page, and participants (properties/customers) are assigned per-group.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

### AC4 — Costs divided by pre-defined percentage shares

1. Click **Customers & Config** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Property Groups**.
3. Click **Manage** on a property group.
4. Set or verify each participant's **Share %** value in the participant table.
5. Click **Save Participants** (only active when the total equals 100%).
6. The system will use these pre-defined percentages when generating billing events for this shared service — each participant's invoice line will reflect their configured proportion of the total cost.

**Confirms requirement:** The saved percentage shares serve as the pre-defined rules that the system uses to split costs among subscribers.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

### AC5 — Each subscriber receives their own invoice line based on payment share

1. Click **Invoices** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Invoice List**.
3. Find and click on an invoice that belongs to a customer who participates in a property group (shared service).
4. In the invoice detail page, scroll to the **Line Items** section.
5. You will see a table of line items. Each subscriber in a shared service arrangement appears as a separate line. The **Description** column includes the participant's share percentage (e.g. "Waste collection — 40% share"), and an information icon appears next to shared service lines.
6. The **Net** and **Gross** columns show the subscriber's allocated amount (their share of the total cost).

**Confirms requirement:** Individual invoice lines per subscriber are visible in the invoice detail, each reflecting that subscriber's payment share.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

### AC6 — Sum of shares must equal exactly 100%; error if not

1. Click **Customers & Config** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Property Groups**.
3. Click **Manage** on a property group.
4. In the participant table, change one or more **Share %** values so the total does not equal 100% (e.g. leave it at 95% or 105%).
5. The banner at the top of the detail panel immediately turns red and shows: "Total: X.XX% ✗ Must equal 100%".
6. The **Save Participants** button becomes disabled — you cannot save an invalid configuration.
7. Additionally, if you attempt to save via the API (i.e., if the check is bypassed), the backend also returns an error message displayed in the red error banner at the top of the page.

**Confirms requirement:** The UI provides both a live visual warning and disables saving until the total equals exactly 100%.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

### AC7 — Default equal split; users can configure alternative distribution rules per agreement

1. Click **Customers & Config** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Property Groups**.
3. Click **Manage** on a property group.
4. To apply the equal default split: click **Equalise**. All participants will receive an equal share (e.g. 33.33%, 33.33%, 33.34% for three participants).
5. To define a custom distribution: manually change the **Share %** values for individual participants to any amounts that sum to 100%.
6. Click **Save Participants** to store the custom rule.

**Confirms requirement:** The Equalise button delivers the system default; manual editing of share percentages per participant delivers user-configurable alternative distributions.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

### AC8 — Invoicing data includes shared service, customer's % share, and total cost

1. Click **Invoices** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Invoice List**.
3. Click on any invoice for a customer who participates in a shared service (property group).
4. In the invoice detail, scroll to the **Line Items** section.
5. For each shared service line item you will see:
   - **Description**: includes the service name and the customer's percentage share (e.g. "Container emptying — 30% share")
   - An information tooltip icon on the line confirming it represents a share of a shared service
   - A sub-line below the description showing: "Service total: X.XXXX" — this is the total cost of the full shared service before the split was applied
   - The **Net** and **Gross** columns show the customer's allocated amount

**Confirms requirement:** The invoice line clearly shows the shared service identity, the customer's percentage share, and the total service cost — all three data points required by this AC.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

### AC9 — Shared services created as property groups; properties added; share percentages defined per participant

1. Click **Customers & Config** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Property Groups**.
3. Click **+ New Group**. Enter the group name (this represents your shared service) and an optional description, then click **Create**.
4. The new group appears in the table. Click **Manage** to open its detail panel.
5. Click **+ Add Row** to add a participant. Enter:
   - **Customer Number** — identifies which customer/property participates
   - **Share %** — their percentage of the service cost (0–100)
   - **Valid From** / **Valid To** — the date range for which this share applies
   - **Include if 0%** checkbox — whether a zero-share participant appears on the invoice
6. Repeat for each participant. Use **Equalise** for an equal split or enter custom percentages.
7. Once the total equals 100%, click **Save Participants**.

**Confirms requirement:** Users can fully create shared services as property groups, add participating properties, and define per-participant share percentages — all through the Property Groups UI.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE
