## Where to Find This Feature

This feature is split across two areas of the app. The global surcharge rules are configured under the **Master Data** tab via the **Surcharge** sidebar item. Individual surcharge removal for a specific invoice is done under the **Invoices** tab by opening any invoice that has a surcharge line item.

## User Flows by Acceptance Criterion

---

### AC1 — Global toggle and per-delivery-method surcharge rules

1. Click **Master Data** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Surcharge**.
3. You will see the **Surcharge Configuration** page. At the top is a banner showing whether invoice surcharges are currently **ENABLED** or **DISABLED** globally, highlighted in green or red respectively.
4. To toggle all surcharges on or off, click **Disable All** (if currently enabled) or **Enable All** (if currently disabled). The banner updates immediately to reflect the new state.
5. Below the banner you will see a table listing all configured surcharge rules. Each row shows the delivery method (E_INVOICE, EMAIL, PAPER, DIRECT_PAYMENT), customer type, amount, and whether the rule is active.
6. To add a new surcharge rule, click the **+ Add Config** button in the top-right of the page.
7. A modal appears where you select the **Delivery Method** (paper, email, e-invoice, or direct payment), enter an **Amount (€)**, and optionally fill in a description.
8. Click **Save**. The new rule appears in the table and will be applied automatically to all invoices for customers whose delivery method matches.

**Confirms requirement:** The global enable/disable toggle and the per-delivery-method rule table confirm that surcharges are method-specific and can be turned off globally.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

### AC2 — Different surcharge amounts per customer type

1. Click **Master Data** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Surcharge**.
3. You will see the surcharge rules table. The **Customer Type** column shows which type each rule applies to (PRIVATE, BUSINESS, MUNICIPALITY, AUTHORITY, or "All" if blank).
4. To add a surcharge that applies only to business customers, click **+ Add Config**.
5. In the modal, select the delivery method (e.g. **PAPER**), then choose **BUSINESS** from the **Customer Type** dropdown.
6. Enter the amount specific to business customers and click **Save**.
7. Repeat the process with **PRIVATE** selected to set a different amount for consumer customers.
8. The table will now show two rows for the same delivery method but different customer types and amounts.

**Confirms requirement:** Multiple rows for the same delivery method with different customer types and amounts demonstrate that surcharge amounts can differ for business vs. consumer customers.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC3 — Separate product per surcharge method; shown automatically; can be switched off per config

1. Click **Master Data** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Surcharge**.
3. Each surcharge rule in the table represents a separate product-like configuration for a specific delivery method (e.g. paper invoice surcharge, email surcharge). These are applied automatically when an invoice is generated for a customer whose delivery method matches.
4. To deactivate a specific surcharge rule without deleting it, click **Edit** on any row. In the modal, uncheck the **Active** checkbox and click **Save**.
5. The table row will show "No" in the **Active** column, meaning that particular surcharge will no longer be added to new invoices.
6. To remove a surcharge from a specific invoice that has already been generated (see AC8), navigate to that invoice via **Invoices** → **Invoice List**.

**Confirms requirement:** The per-rule active toggle and the global toggle together demonstrate that users can switch off surcharges at both the rule level and the system level.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

### AC4 — Situation-specific exceptions can be applied

1. Click **Master Data** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Surcharge**.
3. Click **+ Add Config** (or **Edit** on an existing rule).
4. In the modal, you will see two exception checkboxes:
   - **Exempt first invoice** — when checked, no surcharge is added to the customer's very first invoice.
   - **Require tariff inclusion** — when checked, the surcharge is only applied if the customer has an active contract/tariff that includes it.
5. Check one or both options as appropriate and click **Save**.

**Confirms requirement:** The two exception checkboxes in the add/edit modal cover the main situation-specific exceptions described in the requirement.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

### AC5 — Exempt surcharge from first invoice

1. Click **Master Data** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Surcharge**.
3. Click **+ Add Config** or **Edit** on an existing rule.
4. In the modal, check the checkbox labelled **Exempt first invoice (no surcharge on customer's first invoice)**.
5. Click **Save**.
6. Going forward, this surcharge will not be applied to the first invoice generated for any new customer matching this rule's delivery method and customer type.

**Confirms requirement:** The "Exempt first invoice" checkbox directly implements the first-invoice exemption.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

### AC6 — Prevent surcharge when not included in tariff

1. Click **Master Data** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Surcharge**.
3. Click **+ Add Config** or **Edit** on an existing rule.
4. In the modal, check the checkbox labelled **Require tariff inclusion (only apply if customer has an active contract)**.
5. Click **Save**.
6. This surcharge will now only be applied when the customer's active contract/tariff explicitly includes it.

**Confirms requirement:** The "Require tariff inclusion" checkbox prevents the surcharge from being applied to customers whose tariff does not include it.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

### AC8 — Manually remove individual surcharge from individual invoice

1. Click **Invoices** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Invoice List**.
3. You will see a table of all invoices. Click on any invoice row to open its detail page.
4. If the invoice contains a surcharge line item (identified by a description beginning with "Invoicing surcharge"), a red **Remove Surcharge** button will appear in the top-right action area of the invoice detail page.
5. Click **Remove Surcharge**. A confirmation dialog appears asking "Remove all surcharge line items from this invoice?"
6. Click **OK**. The surcharge line items are removed from the invoice and the page refreshes to show the updated line items without the surcharge.

**Confirms requirement:** The "Remove Surcharge" button on the invoice detail page allows users to manually remove surcharges from individual invoices.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

### AC10 — Waste companies can define when the system does not apply surcharges

1. Click **Master Data** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Surcharge**.
3. Waste companies control surcharge application through three mechanisms visible in the UI:
   - The global **Disable All / Enable All** toggle disables all surcharges system-wide.
   - The **Active** checkbox on each rule deactivates a specific rule.
   - The **Require tariff inclusion** and **Exempt first invoice** checkboxes define condition-based exceptions.
4. Clicking **Edit** on any rule and unchecking **Active** then saving will stop that rule from being applied to new invoices.

**Confirms requirement:** Multiple controls at global, rule, and condition levels give waste companies full control over when surcharges are applied.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE
