## User Flows by Acceptance Criterion

---

### AC1 — Retroactive participant addition redistributes events in date range

1. Click **Customers & Config** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Property Groups**.
3. You will see a table listing all shared service property groups, showing each group's name, number of participants, total share percentage, and whether the shares are valid (summing to 100%).
4. Find the group you want to update and click the **Manage** button in the Actions column.
5. A detail panel opens on the right side of the screen, showing the group name, a validity indicator (green if shares sum to 100%, red otherwise), and a table of current participants with their share percentages and date ranges.
6. Click the **+ Add Retroactively** button in the bottom row of action buttons.
7. A modal dialog titled "Add Participant Retroactively" opens. It contains three fields: **Customer Number**, **Share %**, and **Valid From (historical date)**.
8. The modal explains: "The new participant will be added from the specified date. All events from that date onwards will be redistributed with the updated participant set."
9. Enter the customer's number in the **Customer Number** field, the desired share percentage in **Share %**, and a past date in the **Valid From** field.
10. Click **Add & Redistribute** (the button is disabled until all three fields are filled).
11. You will see a green success message: "Participant added retroactively — affected events have been redistributed." The participant panel refreshes to show the updated participant list.

**Confirms requirement:** The success message and the automatic refresh of the participant list confirm that all events falling within the specified date range have been redistributed based on the updated participation data.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

### AC2 — Shared service events distributed based on participant allocation

1. Click **Customers & Config** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Property Groups**.
3. You will see the list of property groups with a **Total %** column showing the combined share of all participants and a **Valid** column showing a checkmark or cross.
4. Click **Manage** on any group row.
5. The detail panel on the right shows a table with each participant's **Customer Number**, **Share %**, **Valid From**, **Valid To**, and an "Include if 0%" flag — these fields define how billing events are split between participants.
6. You can edit share percentages directly in the table, or click **Equalise** to automatically divide shares equally among all participants.
7. Click **Save Participants** (only enabled when the total equals 100%) to persist the allocation.
8. On a billing event detail page (reached via **Operations** > **Billing Events** > **View**), the field **Shared Collection Group %** shows the percentage assigned to that specific event's participant, confirming the allocation is applied per event.

**Confirms requirement:** The participant allocation table and the per-event "Shared Collection Group %" field confirm that events are dynamically distributed based on participant share percentages.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC3 — New events may be generated when a participant is added retroactively

1. Click **Customers & Config** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Property Groups**.
3. Click **Manage** on a property group row to open the detail panel.
4. Click **+ Add Retroactively**.
5. Fill in the **Customer Number**, **Share %**, and **Valid From** fields (use a past date to cover historical events).
6. Click **Add & Redistribute**.
7. After the success message appears ("Participant added retroactively — affected events have been redistributed"), navigate to **Operations** > **Billing Events** in the top navigation bar.
8. You will see the billing events list. New events may appear for the newly added participant, generated by the redistribution process, covering the date range starting from the historical "Valid From" date.

**Confirms requirement:** The redistribution triggered by the retroactive add causes the backend to generate new billing events for the new participant; these become visible in the Billing Events list.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC4 — Participants can be linked to a shared service retroactively

1. Click **Customers & Config** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Property Groups**.
3. Click **Manage** on the group you want to modify.
4. In the group detail panel, click **+ Add Retroactively**.
5. A modal titled "Add Participant Retroactively" opens with fields for **Customer Number**, **Share %**, and **Valid From (historical date)**.
6. Enter a historical date (any past date) in the **Valid From** field. This links the participant to the shared service as of that past date, making them a retroactive member.
7. Click **Add & Redistribute** to confirm.
8. The participant list refreshes and the new participant appears with the historical "Valid From" date, confirming they are now linked to the shared service from that point in time.

**Confirms requirement:** The "Valid From (historical date)" field and the "Add & Redistribute" action confirm that participants can be linked to a shared service retroactively as members.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE
