## Where to Find This Feature

This feature has two main surfaces. The automated ERP-driven sync is exercised via the **Tools** tab → **Address Sync** page, which lets an operator manually push a billing address update into the invoicing system. The resulting effect on open invoices and payment reminders is visible inside individual invoice detail pages, where a **Payment Reminders** panel shows who will receive reminders and allows sending them.

## User Flows by Acceptance Criterion

---

### AC1 — ERP billing address changes sync to the invoicing system

1. Click **Tools** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Address Sync**.
3. You will see the **Billing Address Sync** page with a form labelled "Push a billing address update from WasteHero to this customer's record."
4. Fill in the **Customer Number** field (required) and update any of the address fields: **Street Address**, **Postal Code**, **City**, **Country Code**, **Alt Street Address**, **Alt City**, **Alt Country Code**, **Email Address**, or **E-Invoicing Address**.
5. Click the **Sync Address** button.
6. A response panel appears showing a coloured status badge (**UPDATED**, **SKIPPED_LOCKED**, or **NOT_FOUND**) and a plain-English message confirming what happened.

**Confirms requirement:** The page accepts incoming address data (representing an ERP push) and applies it to the named customer record, replicating the synchronisation from ERP to invoicing system.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC2 — New billing address applied to all open invoices after sync

1. Follow steps 1–6 in AC1 to sync a billing address.
2. The response shows a status of **UPDATED** when the address was successfully saved to the customer record.
3. Navigate to **Invoices** in the top navigation bar → **Invoice List** in the sidebar.
4. Find any open invoice for the same customer and click its row to open the Invoice Detail page.

**Confirms requirement:** After a successful sync the backend applies the new address to open invoices automatically. However, the Address Sync response panel does not explicitly confirm how many open invoices were updated — it only confirms the customer record was changed. No invoice-level confirmation of the address propagation is shown in the UI.

**UI Status:** ⚠️ PARTIAL

**What is missing:** The Address Sync result message does not tell the user how many (or which) open invoices received the updated billing address. There is no list or count of "invoices updated" shown after the sync completes. Users must manually inspect individual invoice detail pages to verify the address change was applied.

---

### AC3 — Payment reminders sent to the updated address after a change

1. Click **Invoices** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Invoice List**.
3. You will see a table of invoices. Find an invoice with status **SENT** or **COMPLETED** and click its row.
4. The **Invoice Detail** page opens. Scroll down to the **Payment Reminders** section at the bottom of the page.
5. The panel shows a table of existing reminders with columns for **#**, **Status**, **Recipient**, **Method**, **Sent At**, and **Actions**.
6. The **Recipient** column shows the address to which each reminder was or will be sent.
7. To create a new reminder, click **+ New Reminder**, optionally enter a custom message, and click **Create Reminder**.
8. Once a reminder exists with status **PENDING**, click the **Send** button on its row to dispatch it.
9. After sending, the status badge changes to **SENT** and the **Sent At** timestamp is filled in.

**Confirms requirement:** Reminders can be created and sent from the Invoice Detail page. The Recipient column confirms the delivery address, ensuring reminders go to the most recently synced address.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

### AC4 — After manager change, reminders and open invoices go to new manager B

1. First, push the updated billing address for the customer (new manager B's address) via **Tools** → **Address Sync** (see AC1 steps).
2. Confirm the status badge shows **UPDATED**.
3. Then navigate to **Invoices** → **Invoice List** and locate open invoices for that customer.
4. Click an invoice row to open **Invoice Detail**.
5. Scroll to the **Payment Reminders** panel and verify the **Recipient** field shows the new address.
6. Create and send a new reminder using **+ New Reminder** → **Create Reminder** → **Send**.

**Confirms requirement:** The workflow ensures that after the address sync, newly created reminders use the updated address. However, the UI does not automatically display a before/after comparison showing the manager change, and there is no filter or report showing "reminders sent before vs. after manager change."

**UI Status:** ⚠️ PARTIAL

**What is missing:** The UI has no dedicated view showing a manager-change history or confirming that all in-flight reminders targeting the old manager were redirected. Users must manually verify by inspecting reminder recipients on each invoice.

### AC5 — New billing address transferred to invoice operator via the integration interface

1. Click **Tools** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Address Sync**.
3. Fill in the customer number and new address fields and click **Sync Address**.
4. The backend saves the new address to the customer record, from which subsequent invoice transmissions to the operator will use the updated address.
5. To verify operator transmissions, navigate to **Invoices** → **Invoice List**, open an invoice, and scroll to the transmission panel on the Invoice Detail page to see transmit/retransmit controls.

**Confirms requirement:** The Address Sync form directly feeds the integration layer that supplies address data to the invoice operator during transmissions.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

### AC6 — Billing address details updated whenever they change

1. Click **Customers & Config** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Billing Profiles**.
3. You will see a list of customer billing profiles. Click any customer row to open their **Billing Profile** page.
4. The **Profile** tab is shown by default. Scroll down to the **Billing Address** section.
5. Edit any of the fields: **Street Address**, **Postal Code**, **City**, **Country Code**, **Alt Street Address**, **Alt City**, or **Alt Country Code**.
6. Click **Save Billing Profile**.
7. A green success message appears: "Billing profile saved successfully."
8. To review the change history, click the **Change History** tab — a table shows each field changed, old value, new value, who changed it, and when.

**Confirms requirement:** The billing profile form allows address edits, saves them, and records them in an audit log. This covers the manual-update path; the API-driven path is covered by the Address Sync page.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

### AC11 — Update open invoices already transferred to invoice broker

1. Click **Tools** in the top navigation bar → **Address Sync** in the sidebar.
2. Enter the customer number and new billing address and click **Sync Address**.
3. The backend applies the new address to the customer record, including for invoices already transmitted to the broker. The result panel shows **UPDATED** or **SKIPPED_LOCKED**.

**Confirms requirement:** The sync endpoint is designed to reach already-transmitted invoices. However, the UI does not display whether already-transmitted invoices were specifically updated at the broker level — it only confirms the local record was changed.

**UI Status:** ⚠️ PARTIAL

**What is missing:** The sync result does not distinguish between "updated on our side only" and "updated at the invoice broker too." There is no transmit-update or re-push button to explicitly resend the corrected address to the broker for individual invoices.

### AC12 — Payment reminders must go to updated address after hosting agency change

Same as AC3 — payment reminders are created and sent from the **Invoice Detail** page. After a billing address sync, the recipient address on new reminders reflects the updated address.

1. Sync the new hosting agency's address via **Tools** → **Address Sync**.
2. Open any open invoice for that customer via **Invoices** → **Invoice List** → click the invoice row.
3. Scroll to the **Payment Reminders** panel.
4. Click **+ New Reminder** → **Create Reminder**.
5. Click **Send** on the new PENDING reminder.
6. The **Recipient** column on the sent reminder shows the new hosting agency's address.

**Confirms requirement:** Reminders dispatched after the address change go to the updated recipient address.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

## Backend-Only Aspects

The automatic propagation of a newly synced billing address to all open invoices in the database happens entirely in the backend service layer (triggered by the `/api/v1/sync/billing-address` call). There is intentionally no separate screen for this — it is a side effect of the sync action. Similarly, the logic that ensures reminders are re-routed to the updated address when sent is backend-driven; the UI only exposes the "Send" trigger and the resulting recipient in the reminders table.
