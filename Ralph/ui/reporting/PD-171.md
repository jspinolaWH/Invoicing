## Where to Find This Feature

This feature lives under the **Tools** tab in the top navigation bar. It is accessed through the **Authority View** sidebar item, which opens a dedicated read-only invoice viewing screen for authority users.

---

## User Flows by Acceptance Criterion

---

### AC1 — Authorities can independently view invoices sent to customers

1. Click **Tools** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Authority View**.
3. You will see the "Authority Invoice View" page with a yellow badge labelled **AUTHORITY_VIEWER** and a subtitle reading "Read-only access to sent and completed invoices."
4. A filter bar appears at the top with fields for **Customer ID**, **Invoice Date From**, and **Invoice Date To**.
5. Leave all filters blank (or enter a specific customer ID) and click **Search**.
6. A table loads showing all qualifying invoices with columns: Invoice #, Date, Due Date, Customer, Net, Gross, VAT, Type, Status, and Actions.
7. The user can browse and open invoices independently without needing any other screen.

**Confirms requirement:** The authority user can navigate directly to the Authority View page and retrieve a full list of sent invoices without assistance, confirming independent access.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC2 — UI displays the invoice image exactly as sent to the customer

1. Click **Tools** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Authority View**.
3. After the invoice list loads, locate the invoice you wish to inspect.
4. In the **Actions** column of that invoice row, click the blue **View PDF** button.
5. A full-screen modal overlay appears with the title "Invoice PDF."
6. The PDF of the invoice (exactly as it was sent to the customer) is rendered inside the modal using an embedded viewer.
7. Click the **×** button in the top-right corner of the modal to close it.

**Confirms requirement:** The user sees the original invoice PDF rendered in a fullscreen viewer, matching the image sent to the customer.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC3 — Authority can see invoice status and context of inquiries/objections

1. Click **Tools** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Authority View**.
3. Use the **Customer ID** filter to narrow results to a specific customer involved in an objection or waste fee inquiry, then click **Search**.
4. The table displays all matching invoices. The **Status** column shows a colour-coded badge for each invoice (e.g. SENT in purple, COMPLETED in green, CANCELLED in grey).
5. Click anywhere on an invoice row to expand a detail panel below the table showing line items (Description, Quantity, Unit Price, VAT %, Net, Gross, Classification).
6. In the same expanded panel, an **Attachments** section loads automatically, showing any files attached to the invoice (filename, type, size, and a download link).

**Confirms requirement:** The authority user can view both the current status badge and the full line-item breakdown for any invoice, providing context for an objection or waste fee inquiry.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC4 — Authority user opens a customer invoice and checks payment status

1. Click **Tools** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Authority View**.
3. Enter the customer's ID in the **Customer ID** field and click **Search**.
4. You will see a list of invoices for that customer. The **Status** column immediately shows the current status of each invoice (for example, SENT, COMPLETED, or CANCELLED).
5. Click on any invoice row to open the expanded detail panel, which shows line items and attachments.
6. Alternatively, click the blue **View PDF** button in the Actions column to open the original PDF invoice in a full-screen viewer to confirm the exact document the customer received.
7. The status badge remains visible in the table even while the detail panel is open.

**Confirms requirement:** The authority user can see the payment/processing status of any invoice (SENT, COMPLETED, CANCELLED) directly in the table, satisfying the requirement to check invoice status during an inquiry.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE
