## Where to Find This Feature

This feature lives in two places. The gross/net preference is set per customer under the **Customers & Config** tab on the Billing Profile page. The result of that setting — adapted amounts, VAT display, and formatting — is visible on every Invoice Detail page under the **Invoices** tab.

---

## User Flows by Acceptance Criterion

---

### AC1 — Automatic gross or net invoice creation

1. Click **Invoices** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Invoice List**.
3. You will see a table of invoices with columns for Invoice #, Customer, Date, Net, and Gross — both amount columns are always populated for every invoice.
4. Click on any invoice row to open the detail page.
5. At the top of the detail page you will see a **Mode** field showing either `GROSS` or `NET`. This reflects which type of invoice was automatically created.
6. Below the line items you will see an amount summary panel. If the mode is GROSS, the Gross Amount is displayed prominently (larger font, marked "Primary"). If the mode is NET, the Net Amount is displayed prominently.

**Confirms requirement:** The invoice was created automatically with the correct type, and the Mode label and amount summary prove it.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC2 — Gross/net preference stored in customer profile and auto-applied

1. Click **Customers & Config** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Billing Profiles**.
3. You will see a table of customers. Click on any customer row.
4. The Billing Profile page opens. Scroll down to the **Invoicing Mode** field — it shows a dropdown with two options: `GROSS` and `NET`.
5. The current setting saved for this customer is pre-selected.
6. When invoices are generated for this customer (via Generate Invoice or an Invoice Run), the system automatically uses this setting — no manual selection is required at generation time.

**Confirms requirement:** The Invoicing Mode dropdown on the Billing Profile page stores the preference; the Invoice Detail page for this customer's invoices will always reflect the same mode.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC3 — Default gross/net settings for consistency

1. Click **Customers & Config** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Billing Profiles**.
3. Click on any customer row to open their Billing Profile.
4. The **Invoicing Mode** field defaults to `NET` when a new customer profile is created (the form initialises with NET pre-selected).

**Confirms requirement:** There is a consistent per-customer default (NET), which reduces manual adjustments for new customers.

**UI Status:** ⚠️ PARTIAL

There is no company-wide or system-level default configuration screen visible in the UI. The default is hardcoded to NET in the new-profile form. Users cannot see or change a global default — only the per-customer setting.

---

### AC4 — Customer-level override by authorized users

1. Click **Customers & Config** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Billing Profiles**.
3. Click on any customer row to open their Billing Profile.
4. Locate the **Invoicing Mode** dropdown.
5. Change the value from `NET` to `GROSS` (or vice versa).
6. Scroll down and click **Save Billing Profile**.
7. You will see a green success message: "Billing profile saved successfully."

**Confirms requirement:** The override is saved and will be used on all future invoices for this customer.

**UI Status:** ⚠️ PARTIAL

The field is editable by any logged-in user. There is no visible role-based restriction or lock on the Invoicing Mode field in the frontend — any user can change it, not just "authorized personnel" as the requirement states.

---

### AC5 — Automatic VAT calculation displayed based on invoice type

1. Click **Invoices** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Invoice List**.
3. Click any invoice row to open the detail page.
4. Scroll down to the **Line Items** table. Each row shows columns for VAT %, Net amount, and Gross amount. The column header for the primary amount is marked with a blue "PRIMARY" badge — Net if the mode is NET, Gross if the mode is GROSS.
5. Below the line items, the **Amount Summary** panel displays Net Amount, VAT Amount, and Gross Amount side by side. The primary amount is shown in large bold text; the secondary in smaller grey text.

**Confirms requirement:** VAT is calculated and shown for every line item; the amount summary panel makes it clear which type of invoice this is and what the VAT contribution is.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC6 — Invoice template dynamically adapts to net or gross display

1. Click **Invoices** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Invoice List**.
3. Click any invoice row.
4. On the Invoice Detail page, the **Mode** field in the header reflects the customer's Invoicing Mode setting.
5. In the Line Items table, the column header labelled **Net** or **Gross** will display a blue "PRIMARY" badge on whichever column is the primary display amount for this invoice type.
6. In the Amount Summary panel at the bottom, the gross or net figure is styled as the dominant number depending on mode — confirming the template has adapted its display layout automatically.

**Confirms requirement:** The invoice detail page layout adapts its primary amount display based on the customer's gross/net mode — the template formatting is dynamic.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC7 — Data transfer to external invoicing systems includes gross/net metadata

1. Click **Invoices** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Invoice List**.
3. Click any invoice row to open the detail page.
4. Scroll down to the **Attachments** section.
5. Click the **↓ FINVOICE XML** button.
6. The browser will download an XML file containing the invoice's FINVOICE data. This file includes the invoicing mode (gross/net) as part of the invoice metadata exported to the external invoicing system.

**Confirms requirement:** The FINVOICE XML download button is visible on every invoice detail page, and the exported file carries the gross/net metadata.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC8 — Access control: only authorized users can modify gross/net setting

1. Click **Customers & Config** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Billing Profiles**.
3. Click on any customer row to open their Billing Profile.
4. The **Invoicing Mode** dropdown is visible and editable.

**Confirms requirement:** ⚠️ The field is editable in the UI with no visible role-based restriction. The backend may enforce access control, but no front-end indication (disabled state, tooltip, or role check) is shown to restrict who can modify the gross/net setting.

**UI Status:** ⚠️ PARTIAL

The frontend does not apply a role gate or read-only state to the Invoicing Mode field. Any authenticated user who can view a customer's Billing Profile can also change the Invoicing Mode. AC8 may be handled in the backend API, but there is no UI-level enforcement or visual access control.
