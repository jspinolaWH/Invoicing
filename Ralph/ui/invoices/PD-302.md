## Where to Find This Feature

This feature lives primarily under the Invoices tab. Individual custom text is set on the Invoice Detail page, and bulk text is applied from the Invoice List page by selecting multiple invoices and using the Set Text action.

## User Flows by Acceptance Criterion

---

### AC1 — Custom text visible on invoice and included in FINVOICE

1. Click **Invoices** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Invoice List**.
3. You will see a table of all invoices with columns for Invoice #, Customer, Date, Net, Gross, Status, and Billing Type.
4. Click any invoice row (on the invoice number, customer name, or any column except the checkbox) to open the Invoice Detail page.
5. Scroll down the Invoice Detail page. You will see a **Custom Text** section below the line items and amount summary.
6. The label reads "Custom Text (visible to customer)". Any text entered here is displayed on the invoice and is included in the FINVOICE XML export.

**Confirms requirement:** The "Custom Text (visible to customer)" label and the FINVOICE XML download button in the Attachments section confirm the text is surfaced on the invoice and exported in FINVOICE format.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC2 — Custom text input: individual and batch

**Individual:**

1. Click **Invoices** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Invoice List**.
3. Click any invoice row to open the Invoice Detail page.
4. Scroll to the **Custom Text** section.
5. Type your message in the text area labelled "Custom Text (visible to customer)".
6. Click **Save Text**. You will see a brief "Saved." confirmation message next to the button.

**Batch (multiple invoices at once):**

1. Click **Invoices** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Invoice List**.
3. Check the checkbox on the left of one or more invoice rows. (Checkboxes are only enabled for invoices with status SENT or COMPLETED.)
4. A toolbar appears above the table showing the count of selected invoices and three buttons: **Batch Credit**, **Set Text**, and **Clear**.
5. Click **Set Text**.
6. A modal opens titled "Set Custom Text — N invoice(s)". Enter the message in the text area labelled "Custom text (shown on invoice and in FINVOICE)".
7. Click **Apply Text**. You will see a green banner confirming "Custom text applied to N invoice(s)".

**Confirms requirement:** Both individual and batch text input are available and result in confirmation messages.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC3 — Free-text field: dedicated section on the invoice

1. Click **Invoices** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Invoice List**.
3. Click any invoice row to open the Invoice Detail page.
4. Scroll past the Line Items table and Amount Summary.
5. You will see a heading **Custom Text** followed by a text area labelled "Custom Text (visible to customer)" with placeholder text "Optional invoice message or notice...".
6. This is a dedicated free-text section on the invoice for any additional message to the customer.

**Confirms requirement:** The dedicated "Custom Text" section with a labelled text area on the Invoice Detail page satisfies the free-text field requirement.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC4 — Bulk application: mass-apply message to selected invoices

1. Click **Invoices** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Invoice List**.
3. You will see a table of invoices. Each row has a checkbox on the left.
4. Tick the checkboxes on two or more invoice rows (rows with status SENT or COMPLETED have active checkboxes).
5. A selection toolbar appears above the table. It shows the number of selected invoices and includes a **Set Text** button.
6. Click **Set Text**.
7. A modal opens. Enter the message you want applied to all selected invoices in the text area.
8. Click **Apply Text**.
9. A green success banner appears stating how many invoices were updated. The selected checkboxes are cleared.

**Confirms requirement:** The "Set Text" bulk action allows one message to be applied to all selected invoices in a single step.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC5 — Export to FINVOICE format: text included in FINVOICE data

1. Click **Invoices** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Invoice List**.
3. Click any invoice row to open the Invoice Detail page.
4. Scroll down to the **Attachments** section.
5. You will see a button labelled **↓ FINVOICE XML** in the top-right of the Attachments section.
6. Click **↓ FINVOICE XML**. The FINVOICE XML file downloads to your computer as an `.xml` file named after the invoice number.
7. The downloaded XML includes the custom text that was set on the invoice, alongside all other invoice data, ready for transfer to the external invoicing system.

**Confirms requirement:** The FINVOICE XML download button on the Invoice Detail page confirms that the FINVOICE material is accessible. The modal label "Custom text (shown on invoice and in FINVOICE)" also explicitly states inclusion.

**UI Status:** ✅ VISIBLE

---

### AC6 — Audit and accounting: custom text stored as part of invoicing history

1. Click **Invoices** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Invoice List**.
3. Click any invoice row to open the Invoice Detail page.
4. Scroll to the bottom of the page.
5. You will see a **Reporting Data Audit Trail** section. It displays a table with columns: Line Item, Field, Assigned Value, Logged At, and Logged By.
6. This audit trail records changes to reporting fields on invoice line items as part of the invoicing history.

**Confirms requirement (partial):** A reporting data audit trail is present on the Invoice Detail page and records invoicing history. However, this audit trail tracks line-item reporting field changes rather than specifically tracking custom text additions or modifications. There is no dedicated audit log showing when and by whom the custom text was set or changed.

**UI Status:** ⚠️ PARTIAL

**What is missing:** The existing Reporting Data Audit Trail does not explicitly show custom text changes (who set the text, what the previous value was, when it changed). The custom text is saved to the invoice but changes to it are not surfaced in a visible change log in the UI.

---

### AC7 — Seamless template integration: custom text integrates with invoice templates

1. Click **Master Data** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Overview** (or scroll down the Master Data hub).
3. Navigate to **Master Data → Invoice Templates** (accessible via the Master Data hub or directly via the URL path).
4. You will see a table of invoice templates with Name, Code, Number Series, and Created By columns.
5. Templates can be created and edited (Add Template / Edit buttons), with fields for Name, Code, and linked Number Series.

**Confirms requirement (partial):** Invoice templates exist as a managed entity in Master Data. However, the template editor does not expose a custom text field or preview showing how the custom text integrates with the rendered template output. The integration is handled at the backend level (the custom text is embedded in the FINVOICE XML and PDF output using the template), but there is no UI step where a user can see or configure how custom text appears within a specific template.

**UI Status:** ⚠️ PARTIAL

**What is missing:** The Invoice Templates page (Master Data) has no field or preview for custom text. Users cannot see how the text will be positioned or rendered within a specific template from the UI.
